Ingredients

  • 1kg chuck beef chooped into chunks
  • 500g kale
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp black pepper
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup maizemeal
  • 2 cloves fresh garlic
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes

Steps

  1. In a large pot, use the butter to fry the onion and garlic until translucent and fragrant (3 min)
  2. Add the beef chunks and brown the meat (5-7 min)
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes. Occasionally mix them to let them incorperate (5min)
  4. In a large pot, the 2 cups of water to boil
  5. Add the maizemeal to the pot. Keep stirring until the desired consistency is reached
  6. Upon reaching the desired consistency, leave the sadza on low heat for around 5 mins
  7. Spoon the sadza and the stew seperately onto a plate
  8. Serve hot and enjoy
